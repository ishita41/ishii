Welcome to AidSquad

AidSquad is a volunteer based, zero-funds organization that works to get surplus food from restaurants and the community to serve less fortunate people. The idea is to create self-sustained chapters across the world who will look after their local community. And in the process, inspire people around us to give back to those who need it most.

Our Aim

No Wastage of Food

Food loss and waste is a major part of the impact of agriculture on climate change and other environmental issues. . Moreover, food waste that is not handled or reclaimed properly, i.e. through composting, can have many negative environmental consequences.

No One Should be Hungry

Everybody wants to help poor people, but when they think about the huge amount of money one has to spend for a cause, many of them fail to come forward. With ‘AidSquad’ , anybody can help a poor person.We hope that no poor person would go to sleep hungry anywhere in the country.

Spread Happiness

While other people celebrate Valentine's day, Children's day etc, AidSquad wants to celebrate #nohungerday for rest of our lives, where poor people in the country never have to struggle for food.
